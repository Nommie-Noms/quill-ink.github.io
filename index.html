How It Works

1. Send a Text

Text one of our employees.

Please include the product name, The business that sells it and the delivery location.

2. Confirm Order

We’ll reply with your delivery confirmation and price once we have reached the business.

3. Personal Delivery

Our courier picks up and delivers your package and delivers it to your chosen destination.

3a. Collections

Our courier picks up your package and puts it in our storage ready to be collected or for a delivery to be rearranged.

3b. Home Delivery

Our courier can also deliver your item to your home and leave your package in your mail box.

Please ensure there is enough storage in your mail box.

Terms of Use

By using Pickup Point Courier Service, you agree to provide accurate delivery information. We are not responsible for incorrect details provided by the sender.

Food & Drinks need to be delivered on the day the order is placed.

We will advise on the cost of the item in a message with the additional 20% included that can be paid to an employee through the cash payment feature on our phones.

Items requiring a license are collection only as we are required to check your license.
